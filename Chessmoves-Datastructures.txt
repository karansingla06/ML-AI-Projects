Dictionary should be used to store possible chess moves. 
Key-value pairs
keys will be the the pieces like king-white, queen-white etc
and values will have the possible coordinates of the next possible moves.
After every step the dictionary will be updated with the next possible moves.

dic= {"KingWhite":[pm1,pm2, etc...], "KingBlack":[posmove1,posmov2, etc], ...... }
